Table Tennis Tournament 2023

Table Tennis Tournament 2023, sometimes referred to as ping-pong, is a racket sport that originated in tennis but differs in that players play on a stationary table rather than a court. Players work in teams of two and four or individually, taking turns using small rackets to return a light, hollow ball over the table's net and onto the other half of the court.

If they are unable to do so, the other team scores a point. Our matches started from 9 November 2023 and ended by 17 November 2023. we had the grand final in which we enjoyed, some snack party, announced winners and gave prizes to them.
